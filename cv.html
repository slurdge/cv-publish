About

In the field of technology and video games for 20 years, I build and managed various projects, from video games to full video game consoles. After creating game engines and games for Nintendo DS, Wii, Xbox360 and PS3, I created a team of 10 people focusing on emulation technologies at Nintendo. Notably, I created, managed and successfully launched the NES Classic Edition,also known as then ‘NES Mini’ in a very short time frame. Before that, we created the Nintendo DS emulator for WiiU with a full blown suite of tools to aid development. Recently, I went on consulting various companies in the fields of technology, IA and video games. I am always looking for challenging proposals and exciting projects. My skill set includes managing teams of very high level engineers, technical management, project planning and technical expertise, with a breadth and depth of subjects.
